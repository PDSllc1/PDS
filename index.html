Our Services

Comprehensive logistics solutions tailored to meet your business needs

Load Searching & Booking

We find suitable and high-paying loads from load boards and brokers according to truck availability. Our team ensures timely booking to keep trucks moving and profitable.

Route Planning

We plan the most efficient and cost-effective routes for drivers. This helps reduce fuel costs, avoid delays, and ensure on-time delivery.

Driver Coordination

We stay in constant communication with drivers to provide load details, pickup and delivery instructions, and support during transit.

Paperwork & Documentation

We handle all dispatch-related paperwork including rate confirmations, Bills of Lading (BOL), and other shipment documents to ensure smooth operations.

Tracking & Updates

We monitor truck locations in real-time and provide regular updates to brokers and customers for transparency and reliability.

Broker & Customer Communication

We manage negotiations, booking confirmations, and all communication with brokers and customers to maintain strong business relationships.

About Proper Delivery Services

Founded with a commitment to excellence in truck dispatching, our company has grown by providing reliable load management and driver support services. Our focus on transparency, efficiency, and customer satisfaction has helped us build strong relationships with drivers and brokers.

We utilize modern dispatch technology, live tracking systems, and digital documentation to improve efficiency, enhance communication, and provide full visibility of truck movements.

Our dedicated dispatch team works closely with drivers, brokers, and customers to ensure efficient load management, real-time tracking, and reliable transportation services.
